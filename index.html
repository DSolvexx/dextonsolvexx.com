Methodology

The Dexton Solvexx Recruitment Framework

We employ a rigorous seven-stage quality pipeline, ensuring that every placement operates with maximal capacity from day one.

1

Discovery & Alignments

We map out your operational environment, culture profiles, technical requirements, salary bounds, and integration standards.

2

Requirement Engineering

Our technical recruiters build highly detailed functional blueprints containing core performance indexes (KPIs) for targeting.

3

Active Sourcing & Sifting

We execute global target campaigns across our proprietary pre-vetted database and primary channels, narrowing prospects.

4

Vetting & Screen Cycle

Candidates face extensive technical testing, interactive language checks, reference background reviews, and personality matching.

5

Strategic Client Interviews

We introduce only the top-tier candidates (typically a 3:1 ratio) accompanied by complete structural verification reports.

6

Compliance & Offer Management

We structure all cross-border legal documents, execute compliance protocols, and optimize compensation packages.

7

Structured Onboarding Support

We help set up communications channels, remote hardware tracking tools, and provide initial performance management support.

Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate details regarding global compliance, operational structures, cross-border invoicing, and talent retention.

What is Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)?
Recruitment Process Outsourcing is a partnership where a specialized provider manages all or part of a company's internal hiring operations. Unlike transactional staffing, Dexton Solvexx acts as an operational extension of your HR wing, designing custom pipelines, employing cutting-edge recruitment technology, and managing compliance structures to drive talent retention.
How does cross-border contracting and billing operate?
Dexton Solvexx operates as the Employer of Record (EOR) or global contracting vendor depending on the engagement. You receive a single, consolidated monthly business invoice in your local currency. We navigate all international compliance requirements, legal filings, tax compliance systems, and benefits operations in the talent's home region.
Can we transfer remote staff to direct employment?
Yes, our contracts support direct transfer options. Once candidates complete a defined service tenure with Dexton Solvexx, clients can seamlessly transition individuals to internal hiring structures subject to standard transfer agreement rules.
How does Dexton Solvexx handle data security and physical hardware?
Data security is critical. All our operations follow strict ISO 27001 parameters and GDPR legal bounds. We structure comprehensive IP protection clauses directly into our customer agreements. Furthermore, we help source, configure, and ship encrypted, MDM-managed computers directly to your offshore workers.
Are the candidates fluent in professional English?
Absolutely. Sourced candidates face strict verbal, syntactic, and technical communications audits. We only introduce professionals who possess high levels of written and spoken native-equivalent corporate English proficiency, ensuring seamless integration into Western business units.
What time zones can the offshore candidates support?
Our global talent pool allows us to cover any core timezone. We configure our talent to match your exact working hours, providing coverage across US Eastern, Pacific, UK Greenwhich, Australian Eastern, or European Central times.
What happens if a placement does not perform as expected?
We offer a solid replacement guarantee. Every permanent placement includes a 90-day structural guarantee. If a candidate underperforms or leaves during this window, we source, qualify, and integrate a suitable replacement at zero additional recruitment cost.
What typical onboarding periods are required?
Our pre-vetted active network allows us to onboard standard professionals within 1 to 2 weeks. For highly custom, niche executive capabilities or complex engineering setups, sourcing and onboarding typically ranges between 3 to 4 weeks.
Is there a minimum size for offshore remote teams?
No. We construct remote offshore offices ranging from a single specialized software developer to comprehensive 100+ seat multi-functional business divisions. Our models scale dynamically based on your organic structural needs.
How do you maintain candidate retention?
Candidate retention is driven by competitive compensation scaling, structured training, clear career roadmaps, and dedicated local HR managers. Dexton Solvexx maintains regular feedback cycles with remote talent, resulting in a 95% retention rate.
